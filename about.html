Our Story

AP Boys Hostel was established with a vision to provide students with more than just accommodation. We understand the importance of a conducive environment for academic success and personal growth.

Located in the heart of Tinkune, Kathmandu, our hostel offers easy access to major educational institutions while maintaining a peaceful atmosphere for focused study.

We take pride in our well-maintained facilities, professional staff, and commitment to creating a supportive community for our residents.

Rules & Regulations

  • Study hours must be strictly observed from 7 PM to 10 PM daily

  • Maintain cleanliness in rooms and common areas

  • Guest visits are allowed only in common areas during specified hours

  • Return to hostel by 8:00 PM (unless prior permission is obtained)

  • Smoking and alcohol are strictly prohibited within the premises

  • Maintain noise levels conducive to study environment

  • Regular attendance at monthly resident meetings is mandatory
